Problem

Large candle events create a lot of tempting narratives: continuation, exhaustion, reversal, scalp, runner, or no edge. The research surface needed to force those narratives through repeatable event-study evidence.

Solution

The studio wraps the analysis runner in a Flask dashboard, streams long execution logs over server-sent events, loads result artifacts, and organizes outputs into overview, rankings, target curves, context effects, and strategy blueprint tabs.

What It Proves

  • Interactive control over event-study parameters without losing reproducibility.
  • Integration with downstream report sections and strategy-construction outputs.
  • Clear separation between configuration, long-running compute, parsed results, and UI rendering.
  • Safety-first public packaging for trading research artifacts.

Redaction Note

Public material shows the application shell and sanitized workflow framing. Raw market data, local data-root paths, large result files, generated strategy templates, and unpublished research conclusions are withheld.
