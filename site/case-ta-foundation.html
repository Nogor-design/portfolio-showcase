TA Foundation Trading Intelligence Suite

A local trading research and analytics platform that ingests NinjaTrader exports, market data, and research artifacts, then produces auditable reports, optimizer decision packages, and validation workflows.

Why It Is Split Into Demos

TA Foundation is too large to present as one portfolio item. It is stronger as several focused case studies: report generation, strategy discovery, optimizer packaging, market-data diagnostics, exit-policy simulation, and research intake.

Best Public Slices

  • Backtest Intelligence Reports: KPI cards, daily outcomes, drawdown, settings, and executive cards.
  • Strategy Discovery Workbench: ranked candidates, MAE/MFE, OOS/holdout, entry/filter/exit evidence.
  • Weekly Coverage Optimizer: lane-grid package, final review, report, and manifest overview.
  • Exit Policy Lab: fixed stops, ATR trails, breakeven, chandelier, and trade debug evidence.
  • Market Data Diagnostics: freshness, tick streams, row counts, and minute-vs-tick checks.

What It Proves

  • Large-system architecture across CLI, web, reports, market data, optimizers, and external tools.
  • Evidence-first analysis: report sections, validation gates, and manifest outputs.
  • Practical orchestration around long-running NinjaTrader and optimizer workflows.
  • Awareness of IP boundaries: public artifacts can show engineering without exposing strategy logic.

Current Evidence

The capability catalog identifies shipped report generation, strategy discovery, prediction, strategy template building, optimizer, weekly coverage, deployment matrix, execution bridge, stop engine/test harness, and market-data surfaces. The analysis guide documents 129 importable report sections, strategy discovery sections, large-candle sections, exit-policy sections, tick diagnostics, optimizer overview, and report presets.

Redaction Note

Trading-related case studies are shown as software engineering and analytics systems. Public demos use sanitized or generated artifacts and omit proprietary strategy rules, account data, broker identifiers, template names, and live trading recommendations.
