Problem

The client needed a credible public website for a trading education business, with clear product positioning, a professional visual system, launch-ready content structure, and room for subscriptions and gated member content.

Solution

The site uses Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind, and reusable UI primitives to organize the homepage, indicators, video library, education, live room, pricing, account pages, and legal disclaimer surfaces.

Where This Fits

Forward this when a client needs a credible product or service site that can launch before every future integration exists. It shows client-facing delivery, conversion path thinking, domain-sensitive copy, and an app structure ready for auth, billing, and member content.

What It Proves

  • Client-facing product-site delivery with a coherent brand and conversion flow.
  • Reusable app structure for future Supabase authentication and Stripe billing.
  • Careful trading-domain copy with education and risk-disclaimer boundaries.
  • Ability to move from idea and roadmap into a concrete launch surface quickly.
