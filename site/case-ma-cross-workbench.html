MA-Cross Workbench

A local research app that turns moving-average ideas into an honest workflow: inspect the signal, run cost-aware discovery, then require cross-instrument proof before treating anything as a candidate edge.

Problem

Strategy research can produce attractive charts and fragile backtest numbers. The workflow needed a way to make hypothesis review visible, cost-aware, and resistant to small-sample or overfit conclusions.

Solution

The workbench is a thin Flask UI over existing `ta_foundation` engines: MA feature generation, signal detection, entry timing, outcome simulation, discovery sweeps, and cross-instrument robustness evaluation.

What It Proves

  • Research UI design that follows the actual analytical pipeline.
  • Cost-aware evaluation with commission, slippage, fill rate, and trade count visible.
  • Out-of-sample degradation and hardening metadata near the leaderboard, not hidden in notes.
  • Cross-instrument proof across independent futures instruments as an anti-overfit gate.
