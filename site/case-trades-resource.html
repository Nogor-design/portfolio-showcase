Trades Resource Command Center

A staffing operations dashboard that turns assignment endings, open orders, recruiter follow-up, candidate readiness, and customer import review into one daily command view.

Problem

Small staffing teams often operate across spreadsheets, paper notes, and memory. The result is missed renewal conversations, slow follow-up, and unclear ownership.

Solution

The command center gives the owner and recruiters a focused daily workflow: Owner Today View, Assignment Forecast, Open Orders Board, Recruiter Process Tracker, and staged customer import review.

What It Proves

  • Workflow analysis for a real business domain.
  • Operational dashboard design around repeated daily decisions.
  • Privacy-aware customer upload/session handling.
  • Normalization of messy staffing files into product views.

Verification

Current audit: required dependencies import, runtime/private folders are ignored by Git, and the public screenshot was captured from a sanitized runtime copy. Demo Data and Client Demo Mode were visible; Full Prototype Mode and Data/Admin were absent.

Redaction Note

Public materials use generated demo data only. Real customer spreadsheets, PDFs, candidates, clients, names, phone numbers, emails, and upload folders are excluded.
